Established since 2004

(Charlotte County Inline Hockey Association)

Coaching kids in hockey and life to instill values such as:

  • Responsibility
  • Leadership
  • Respect
  • Social skills
  • Teamwork

Member of Charlotte County Youth Sports Council

Since our begginings in local parking lots (Walmart, Kmart, YMCA) to the building of our current hockey rink in 2004 and still going strong. We have been providing a friendly and competetive league for all age groups and skill levels at our local hockey rink in Tringali Park in Charlotte County. The only outdoor public hockey rink within 50 miles. We are currently at a fraction of the size we used to be but hoping to regain our former glory. Come play some hockey and help our league grow!!
